It works great for me! Just my two cents:

* Have you considered using a Calendar control for the dates? With your
current UI, users can enter invalid dates, such as 31/February.

progress bar or the names of the files being loaded.

Excellent work. Don't hesitate to announce your new application to the
SIOC community.
