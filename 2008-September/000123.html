Hi everyone,

Last month I attended the "Enhanced Messaging Workshop @ AAAI'08" [1]. 
This was a very good opportunity to meet individuals and groups that are 
pursuing work in this research area. The presentations given at the 
workshop (including ours) are available at the workshop's website.
As a followup to this workshop, the organisers set up a mailing list [2] 
as well as a website [3] for those interested in the latest email research:
"Our intention is for this list to be a tool for the community to 
discuss ideas and projects and to announce resources of interest"
"The website is community maintained and will keep a list of email 
datasets, a bibliography and other information"

Since the intention was to collect all people working in the area in one 
place, I would like to encourage interested individuals to join the 
given mailing list/group.

Thanks,
