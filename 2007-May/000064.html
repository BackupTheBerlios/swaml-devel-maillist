Hi,

The last revision of SIOC ontology official specification [1] now
includes the two properties specified in SWAML ontology [2], with the
consequent style adaptation. It has been a subject discussed in the last
weeks on the SIOC-Dev mailing list [3] (and here), and finally the
properties join into the core specification of SIOC.

I think is a great news for all the people involved in the project. Now
I remember the day in which the relation of the project with SIOC began
[4].

In the next week I'll try to update all the software tools to be
compatible with SIOC 1.22 specification.
