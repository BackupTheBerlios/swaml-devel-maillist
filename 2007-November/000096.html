Call for papers



The 2008 International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Pattern
Recognition (AIPR-08) and the 2008 International Conference on Enterprise
Information Systems and Web Technologies (EISWT-08) (website:
www.PromoteResearch.org <http://www.promoteresearch.org/> ) will be held
during July 7-10 2008 in Orlando, FL, USA. We invite draft paper submissions
and session proposals. The conferences will be held at the same time and
place where several other major events are taking place. The website
contains more details.



Both these conferences particularly encourage paper submissions on all
aspects related to the semantic web!
