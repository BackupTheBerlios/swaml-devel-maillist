IICAI-09 Call for papers: Special session on Semantic Web and Information Retrieval 
 
There is a Special session on Semantic Web and Information Retrieval at the 4th Indian International Conference on Artificial Intelligence (IICAI-09) will be held in Tumkur (near Bangalore), India during December 16-18 2009..
