Yes, indeed I just finished to adapt SWAML to implement SIOC ontology;
but first, before merging branch, I would like to know your opinion
about one detail:

Now, with the work made this week, SWAML are using only two property of
our ontology [1]:
	- swaml:previousByDate
	- swaml:nextByDate

These two properties haven't equivalent [2] in SIOC [3]. Then, not to
lose this important semantic relationship, obviously we must continue
using our ontology.

But my doubt is how:
    a) 	with actual ontology, without any change
    b) 	with a new release of our ontology deleting deprecated 
	properties
    c) 	with a new release of our ontology adding owl:equivalentTo 
	to reference SIOC on this deprecated properties

Perhaps the last one is most logical, but I haven't any experience about
these questions and I don't want to take a wrong decision.
