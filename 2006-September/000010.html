>     b) 	with a new release of our ontology deleting deprecated

I think b) is the most appropriate, but you should delete also the
deprecated classes. In fact, your ontology should contain just these two
properties.

I also think that these properties are useful, and maybe they can be
part of the next release of the SIOC ontology (you can contribute them
to the SIOC project to get feedback). However, there is a problem: you
can make such relationship between messages _in just one Forum_, but if
the messages are published in more than one forum, the relationship may
not hold.
